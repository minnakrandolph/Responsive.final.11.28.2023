Top 3 favorite hikes

Shirly Chisholm State Park

A lively walk hike or fun biking day with rentals available year round. The park features both smooth biking paths and hills for dirt bike terrain, and an overview of Jamaica bay and bird sanctuary.

Address:

J4WG+5M, 1750 Granville Payne Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11239

What I like about it

This was land re-designed from decades of city dump conditions to a thriving green space for the community and ecosystem.

Learn more

Mt.Beacon

No car? No Worries! The Metro North Hudson line will get you from Grand Central to Beacon in just a short trip, with just a short walk that takes you to the main trail! !

Address:

Beacon, NY 12508

What I like about it

The trail takes you to a firetower overlooking one of the highest points in the region of the state!
